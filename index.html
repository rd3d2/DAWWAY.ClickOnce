DAWWAY Lite



"I used to play in bands back in the 80s, and after I retired from computer programming I got back into writing and recording. I found Modern DAWs to be incredible, but once I started using a control surface seriously, I hit a constant annoyance: my hands were on the faders, but my eyes had to keep jumping to the computer screen to see what the DAW was showing. I knew that there had to be a better software solution. I asked around - even spoke to DAW and control surface manufacturers - and everyone liked the idea, but nobody was building it."

So I did.


What DAWWAY Lite does

A dedicated, always-visible meter bridge designed for control-surface workflows.

DAWWAY is a dedicated, always-visible meter bridge for your DAW and your Control Surface. It brings the essential mixer information into the same place as your control-surface workflow - so you make faster decisions, stay in the creative flow, have more control, and your setup feels more like a real console. And this isn’t my first try - it’s the fourth iteration, built from real use, getting closer to the vision… and then going beyond it.

DAWWAY is designed to run on all DAWs and has been tested on:

How it works

Three steps:
  1. Connect DAWWAY ports in your DAW. *
  2. Run DAWWAY Lite and choose your surface & extenders.
  3. Place the Fader Bank windows on any monitor (or touchscreen) at your eyeline and directly above your individual Control Surface Channels

From then on it’s setup once, launch and go.


* Note that if Windows Midi Services 2.0 is not released at NAMM 2026 you will first need to setup virtual midi ports for DAWWAY to use. Full instructions will be provided in the support forum.
